I've everything (un)planned

For all I knew, we were going right,
Said the people and the world,
Said we’ll be happy and pacified,
Yeah sang a song and twirled!

Maybe you think you got this,
But what if it’s not what you want?
This life is like a sad poem,
And on that I’m just a plain font.

Now that I’m here I realise,
I don’t really feel a connection,
I mean isn’t that odd?
It’s like wandering in wrong direction.

I wish I knew what I wanted,
Like something which I can own,
Something which doesn’t feel wrong,
Something for lifelong I’ve known.

Your wrinkles and greys don’t decide,
Whenever you’re ready, it’s time,
I won’t be a confused brat forever,
Someday my mind and work will rhyme.
