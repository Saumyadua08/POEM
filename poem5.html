THE PHASE

Those who are left with bare remains,
Those are the only ones who lost more, less gains.
Maybe I’m one of those, maybe you too,
Does it hurt? When everyone expects best out of you.

Like how does one even cope with that?
You feel hollow despite whatever you get.
I don’t believe in unicorns and rainbows,
I’m just watching the stream of time as it goes.

Everyone seems so happy and satisfied,
I wish I could be too putting all my negatives aside.
Why is it so absurd and important?
I take joy in life as a supplement.
