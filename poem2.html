Diary for you

There’s one I know with something inside,
Not everyone gets it,
Some thoughts, Some words
, Maybe here it doesn’t really fit.

Everyone knows the story of black-sheep,
This sheep wants to become a free bird,
But there’s a constant alarm,
Fear of being known and heard.

There’s gotta be some place,
Where do all boiling thoughts go?
Keep ‘em safe somewhere,
Or write ‘em down for the world to know.
